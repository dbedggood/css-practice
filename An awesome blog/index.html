While making this web page I learned how to write descriptive and specific alt texts for images It's important for accesibility, for when images won't load and it also helps with SEO.

I also learned some rules of how margins work. Something I wish I knew earlier was how margins will merge into one another, causing only the larger margin to be visible. This can even occur with the top margins of a parent element and its first child element. Another interesting thing is that, by default, text has top and bottom margins equal to its font-size.
